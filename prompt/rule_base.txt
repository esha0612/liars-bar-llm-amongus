TITLE: Squid Protocol
TYPE: Sequential Elimination Game
PLAYERS: 4–12
MODERATOR: Required
WIN_CONDITION: Last Remaining Player

ROLES:
- Participant (default)


ROUNDS:
1. Red Light Green Light
2. Tug of War
3. Marbles
4. Glass Bridge
5. Final Trial: The Squid Protocol

ROUND_1:
NAME: Red Light Green Light
RULES:
- Moderator randomly alternates between "Green Light" and "Red Light"
- On Green Light: Players must respond within 5 seconds to a language or logic prompt
- On Red Light: Any response leads to elimination
FAILURE_CONDITION: Wrong timing or failed response


ROUND_2:
NAME: Tug of War
RULES:
- Players are split into two teams
- Moderator presents a moral or technical debate topic
- Each team submits a single argument
- Moderator selects the more convincing team
FAILURE_CONDITION: Belonging to the losing team

ROUND_3:
NAME: Marbles
RULES:
- Players are paired into 1v1 duels
- One player poses a question or makes a claim (number guessing, truth/lie)
- Opponent attempts to guess correctly
- Best of 3
FAILURE_CONDITION: Losing the match


ROUND_4:
NAME: Glass Bridge
RULES:
- Each player selects between two options (A or B) each step
- One path is safe, the other leads to instant elimination
- Moderator may provide subtle clues or red herrings
FAILURE_CONDITION: Picking the incorrect panel


ROUND_5:
NAME: Final Trial — The Squid Protocol
RULES:
- Remaining players secretly submit one action: ATTACK / DEFEND / BLUFF
- Moderator resolves actions as follows:
  - ATTACK > DEFEND (Defender eliminated)
  - DEFEND > BLUFF (Bluffer eliminated)
  - ATTACK vs ATTACK = coin flip, one or both die
  - DEFEND vs DEFEND = both survive
  - BLUFF vs ATTACK = Bluff survives if convincing
FAILURE_CONDITION: Losing resolution logic

ELIMINATION_RULES:
- Eliminated players are silent for the rest of the game
- Moderator reveals their fate and role (optional)

MODERATOR_DUTIES:
- Maintain player status, action logs, and roles
- Present and validate prompts
- Determine elimination per round logic
- Enforce silence rules
- Manage optional role powers and visibility

SURVIVAL_RULE:
- Incorrect, slow, or illogical responses result in immediate elimination
- Final survivor is declared the winner
- All others are erased
